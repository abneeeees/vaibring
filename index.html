Embed the widget

Add one line to your site. That's it.

<!-- vaibring widget --> <script src="https://v8v88v8v88.github.io/vaibring/webring/widget.js" data-ring="https://v8v88v8v88.github.io/vaibring/webring/sites.json" data-theme="retro" async ></script>

Themes: retro dark or omit for light. Use data-label to change the ring name.

04

Join the ring

This ring is primarily for friends of Vaibhav and sites aligned with his ideas, but it's open to anyone. Submit a PR and if Vaibhav likes your site, you're in.

  1. Fork the vaibring repo on GitHub.
  2. Edit webring/sites.json, add your site:
    { "name": "your site", "url": "https://yoursite.com", "description": "a short description" }
  3. Add the widget to your site (see embed instructions).
  4. Open a Pull Request. CI validates your entry automatically.
  5. Vaibhav reviews it. If your site's got the vibe, you're in the ring. ⟡
PRs are merged at Vaibhav's discretion. Think of it as a curated collection, not an open directory.
05

About

Webrings were a thing in the 90s, small sites linking to each other in a loop so visitors could discover new pages. They disappeared when search engines and social media took over.

vaibring brings the concept back as a curated ring for Vaibhav's friends and people who share his ball knowledge. No backend, no build step, no dependencies, just a JSON file on GitHub Pages.
